Who are we

We are a non profit organization aiming to help less fortunate people to see modern art without any need for status or wealth. We decided to form adter the Christchurch Earthquake left so many without home, jobs and family. Hence, a free exhibition to help take the mind off was the only logical option. All art is free and can be viewed either in person at Hagley Park or online
